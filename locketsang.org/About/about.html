About Me

Origin Where it all began

Hey! Nice to meet you!

My name's Locke Tsang and I board in the UK as a Year 13 international student. I grew up in Hong Kong, where I got into engineering early—building a water-powered cruise ship (which sank like the Titanic) and designing LEGO bridges at home.

During COVID, I stumbled into C programming and Unity, which sparked my love for logic and design. Now in Sixth Form, I've dived into robotics—teaching myself Solid Edge CAD and writing multi-file code in different languages. Everything I do is project-based and self-taught.

Discipline Beyond the code

Outside of robotics I participate in competitive fencing. I have been playing fencing since year 4 and has gotten several award throughout my journey from club competitions to national level. I recently started running and have made my own training plan and has been refining to reach my 3:30/km for 5km. I also do plyometrics and strength training as a part of injury prevention and in general burning some time.

Let's Connect Ready to build something amazing?

I made this site to share what I've built and connect with new people. Whether you're looking for a passionate engineer, a creative problem-solver, or just want to chat about robotics and innovation, I'd love to hear from you!

If you're new to here, Welcome, and I look forward to meeting you!
